What is YCE?

YCE is an upcoming festival that involves an epic week of skiing, raving and exploring a breathtaking ski resort in the French Alps.

World class acts by the slopes

Our state of the art main stage is one-of-a-kind and will host some of the biggest acts in the world

Location

YCE is taking place in Avoriaz, the highest resort in the Franco-Swiss region of Portes du Soleil that offers no less than the largest connected ski area in the world, as well as jaw dropping landscapes, plenty of off-piste activities and a broad selection of excellent restaurants, pubs and spas.
